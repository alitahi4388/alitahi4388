About Us

Poultry farms are facilities where chickens are raised for meat. They include housing for chickens, feeding and watering systems, healthcare measures, and waste management. Broiler chickens are raised for meat. Poultry farming is subject to regulations and plays a significant role in the agricultural industry, providing food and economic contributions.
